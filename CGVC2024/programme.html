  • Keynotes from Petra Isenberg and Rachel McDonnell and Amir Hussain (CEO of Yemetech).
  • Special session on Human-centred Decision-Making through Data Visualisation, with talks on cutting-edge EPSRC-funded visualisation research across the UK.
  • Early career researchers are invited to a Research Development fireside chat, where they can have relaxed and friendly conversations with established academics in the field of visualisation and visual computing.
